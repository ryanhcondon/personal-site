About

I'm currently competing as a professional Magic: The Gathering player with team Sanctum of All, and writing about strategy and decision-making on Patreon. I previously spent four years at Qeepsake leading growth marketing and later building the company's data infrastructure as a Business Intelligence Architect, and before that, worked at Wayfair for a year as a marketing data analyst. I have a degree in (and passion for!) mathematics from Washington University in St. Louis, with minors in computer science and psychology.

I'm looking for work in a high-agency, intellectually engaging environment that aligns with my ethics and allows me the flexibility to do deep, thoughtful work.

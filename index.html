About Me!

Hi! I'm Ryan (I use they/them pronouns, but also comfortable with he or she!).

I'm currently competing as a professional Magic: The Gathering player with team Sanctum of All, and writing about strategy and decision-making on Patreon. I previously spent four years at Qeepsake leading growth marketing and later building the company's data infrastructure as a Business Intelligence Architect. I have a degree in (and passion for!) mathematics from Washington University in St. Louis, with minors in computer science and psychology.

I'm looking for work in a high-agency, intellectually engaging environment that aligns with my ethics and allows me the flexibility to do deep, thoughtful work.
